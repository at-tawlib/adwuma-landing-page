About

This project Adwuma has been something I have been thinking of for sometime now. When I moved to a new location, I had an electrical problem and wanted and electrician to fix it for me. The problem was that, the only electrician I knew lived in my former location and where I live now is very far from him. Then I went online to search for an electrician but I could not find any. The search results were fishy and vague. I knew something had to be done but I was not having the time to be commited to it hence I ignored it.

In August 2022, I gained admission to read Software Engineering at ALX an online university. For our Foundations final project, we had to build a portfolio project. This was in June, 2023. I could not think of any project to work on but suddenly, I remembered about my electrical problem. I then decided to use this opportunity to work on the idea I abandoned.
